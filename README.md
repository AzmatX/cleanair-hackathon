## 🌍 Overview

CleanAir AI is an AI-powered citizen reporting and municipal intelligence platform that helps detect, map, predict, and prioritize urban pollution using Google AI and Google Cloud technologies.

Citizens can report pollution by uploading a photo and location. Gemini AI analyzes the image, classifies pollution, estimates severity, and generates actionable insights. The platform visualizes pollution hotspots on Google Maps, predicts future AQI trends, and provides decision support for municipal authorities.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Urban pollution complaints are scattered across phone calls, WhatsApp, social media, and manual registers.

Authorities struggle to:

- Prioritize high-impact complaints
- Detect recurring pollution hotspots
- Predict future pollution risks
- Allocate field inspection teams efficiently

CleanAir AI transforms unstructured citizen reports into actionable intelligence.
